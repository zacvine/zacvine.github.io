My ideal job

My ideal job is as a Financial planner, preferably one who wpecialises in dealing with high net worth clients. A Financial planner is a person who helps an individual or a company to achieve their financial goals, short, medium and long term, through recommending and implementing various investment and saving plans. They often take an holistic approach and also advise in other areas such as insurance, tax, retirement and estate planning. This role appeals to me because of my background in finance and insurance in non-advised roles and my desire to help people and build strong relationships. This role would also suit me due to the fast paced enviroment and the fact that I am money motivated.

To secure and excel in a position such as this I will need several years experience in the financial planning industry, a recognised financial degree and to become a certified financial planner (CFP, as illustrated in the below advertisement.). I would also need experience dealing with high net worth (HNW) clients and experience offering advice using a holistic approach (as explained above.).
