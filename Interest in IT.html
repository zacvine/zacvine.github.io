What is your interest in IT? When did your interest in IT start? Was there a particular event or person that sparked your interest? Outline your IT experience (if any).
My interest in IT was initially fueled many years ago by gaming. I found the PC to be consistently superior to even the latest consoles. This resulted in me building my own gaming rigs and trying to squeeze from them everything I could…overclocking video cards, building water cooling systems to accommodate tweaked processors, custom lighting and cable sleeving to get that sleek gaming look. I developed such a passion for building PC’s that I briefly ran my own business doing so, but unfortunately, I didn’t have the means to compete with the larger scale companies in what was, at the time, quite a specialized market. More recently I have combined my gaming rig and HTPC (Home Theatre Personal Computer) into a single unit.

Why did you choose to come to RMIT?
I chose RMIT because it offers the degree in financial planning I want to complete so I can begin my career as a financial advisor. I am completing this degree through Open Universities as on-line learning allows me to balance my studies with being a fulltime stay at home dad.

3

What do you expect to learn during your studies?
Although the only IT related unit I will be doing during this degree is an introduction, in 3 short weeks I have decided that my initial belief that the unit would be irrelevant was a total misconception on my part. I now believe, and hope, that I will learn a lot during this unit which can be applicable to not just a pursuit in finance, but a pursuit in just about any career. Things like working productively in a group, utilizing various lines of communication and team problem solving. For example, in 2 short weeks I have learnt about Discord for group communication, Prezi for creating presentations and GitHub, where I have put this very web page. I also think that as someone who has stepped away from distance learning for a long time, this unit will rekindle my necessary IT skills to allow me to excel in my studies. Through things like collaboration and observation of other students, I believe I will learn a lot about HOW to learn in what is, for me, a new technological age.
